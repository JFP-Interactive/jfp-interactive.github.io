About Us

We're JFP Interactive, a compact team of three students—Jason, Felix, and Phil—united by a shared journey into the realm of game development, driven by our collective passion and creativity. Our goal is to craft stunning games that captivate with their visuals and auditory experiences.
While we currently participate mainly in game jams, harnessing them as a platform to refine our skills and express our innovative ideas, we're also excitedly working on our first major project.
